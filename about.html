About Wepngong Maureen

My Journey to Making Cardano Governance Accessible

My Story

Hello, I'm Wepngong Maureen. My journey into the world of Cardano governance began with just 39 ADA and a burning question: why does governance feel so inaccessible to the average ADA holder? It seemed like only those with vast amounts of ADA or deep technical knowledge had a voice, leaving many feeling excluded. I started this journey because I believe governance should be for everyone, not just the elite. My initial lack of stake presented a significant challenge, as I needed 500 ADA to even register as a DRep. However, the incredible Cardano community rallied behind my vision, and together we surpassed that goal, reaching 734 ADA! This experience solidified my commitment to representing the stakeless and ensuring that every voice, regardless of ADA balance, is heard and valued. I am living proof that passion and community support can overcome financial barriers in the Cardano ecosystem. I am dedicated to breaking down complex governance jargon, making participation fun and engaging, and creating a safe space for everyone to ask questions and contribute.

The Stakeless Manifesto

Governance should be for everyone—not just for the governance elites, not just for the rich, not just for those who were here since the Byron era.

We are here to build bridges for the stakeless, voiceless, adaless, and those who don't speak English fluently. We believe in a future where everyone can participate in Cardano governance, regardless of their background or resources.
