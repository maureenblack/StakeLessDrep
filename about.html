From 39 ADA to the DRep Seat

A Governance Struggle Story

You ever walk into a room and immediately realize you don't belong here?

Maybe it's a fancy restaurant where the waiter gives you "the look." Maybe it's a Cardano governance discussion where everyone is throwing around terms like "quadratic voting" and you're just trying to figure out where the exit is.

Yeah. Been there.

The Beginning

Started with just 39 ADA and a dream to make governance accessible for everyone. No stake, but a whole lot of determination.

The Challenge

Discovered I needed 500 ADA to register as a DRep. It felt like hitting a wall, but sometimes walls are just doors we haven't figured out how to open yet.

The Community Support

The Cardano community showed up in ways I never expected. Together, we reached 734 ADA! Yes, I cried. No, I'm not ashamed to admit it.

The Mission

Now, as your Stakeless DRep, I'm here to make sure every voice matters. Because governance shouldn't feel like a secret club.

The Stakeless Manifesto

Governance should be for everyone—not just for the governance elites, not just for the rich, not just for those who were here since the Byron era.

Making Governance:

  • Understandable – No more governance jargon walls
  • Accessible – Decision-making open to all
  • Fun – If it feels like homework, people won't do it
  • Safe – No one should feel dumb for asking questions

"The Stakeless will no longer be ignored. We move Tomo ni mae e (forward together). Even if we trip a little along the way."
