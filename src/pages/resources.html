Getting Started with Governance

A DRep (Delegated Representative) is a community member who participates in Cardano's governance by voting on proposals on behalf of ADA holders who delegate their voting power to them.

You can delegate your voting power using compatible wallets. Simply copy the DRep ID and paste it in your wallet's delegation section.
