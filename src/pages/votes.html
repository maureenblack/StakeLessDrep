Current Vote: Cardano Constitution

Voted: Yes February 2025

Rationale

As a developer and constitutional delegate, I understand the urge to keep refining until everything is perfect. However, progress demands action. The interim constitution has served its purpose, but we need a framework that can evolve with our community.

While there are areas that need refinement, particularly in governance processes and implementation details, the built-in amendment process and planned 2025 workshops demonstrate our commitment to continuous improvement. I voted yes in Nairobi because I believed in our potential, and I'm voting yes again because I believe in our ability to grow together.

We're building something unprecedented in blockchain governance. Perfect shouldn't be the enemy of progress, especially when our current framework is reaching its limits. Moving forward together is better than standing still in pursuit of perfection.
